Winnitron Resurrection Jam: Halifax

The Winnitron is being resurrected and we need new games to put on the new Winnitron launcher and are holding a game jam to celebrate!

Bring your laptop, creativity, positive energy, and make games with us! Halifax's Winnitron, The Halifaxmachine, will be there and you can test your games right on the machine itself!

If you're unable to make it to the jam, consider coming on Sunday around 5pm for the presentations of the games that were made over the weekend.

Winnipeg is holding a jam at the same time if you are reading this and live in Manitoba, go register and hang out there! We're sharing some resourses with the folks there, specifically, the Itch.io page where the games will be submitted.

THEME

"Resurrection" or "Land & Sea"

"Resurrection" because the Winnitron is being raised from the dead. "Land & Sea" because the parallel jam in Winnipeg is landlocked, and Halifax is on the ocean!

Use one, both, or neither of the themes however you like, or make something totally different, it's just to get the creative juices flowing. :)


BEFORE YOU JAM

Games for the Winnitron must adhere to a few format rules. Please familiarize yourself with the rules on this forum post.

Sign up for a Winnitron Account for posting your progress and getting feedback as the jam goes on.

Sign up for an Itch.io Account for posting your final game for others to review. We'll be sharing the jam with the Winnipeg Game Jam.
